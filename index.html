What is KALPANA?

KALPANA is named after Kalpana Chawla who was the first woman of Indian origin to travel into space on NASA’s space shuttle mission, Columbia. Although we lost her in a tragic Space Shuttle disaster, she still remains an epitome of inspiration and motivation for all the men and women around the world and especially for the aspiring youth of India.

The Women In Engineering [WIE] Chapter at IEEE PESIT South Student Branch presents to you a one day event which aims to bring Technology and Humanitarianism under one roof. This event consists of two main facets:-

Technology :
This facet of Kalpana consists of engineering students from various colleges across India to showcase their projects which solve real world problems currently existing in the society today. A stringent selection process will shortlist the best ideas/teams who can participate at this project exhibition. Since we are also supporting more women to enter into the field of Science, there will be a criteria of having at least 1 female member per team.

Humanitarianism :
With 47 million young men and women around the world dropping out of school by 10th grade, this facet is aimed at inviting students from underdeveloped government schools across Bangalore to provide them with a talk by appropriate speakers regarding their career and future. We wish is done in order to motivate them to complete their formal education even after high school. [This will be an early morning session conducted by the branch before the competition begins].

We at IEEE PESIT South Student Branch believe that we have a moral responsibility as future engineers to give back to the community we live in. We hope that you believe in the same and participate in Kalpana, 2017.

Steps for students:

Step 1 : Find a team. The maximum number of members per team is 3 with a strict criteria of having at least 1 female member per team.

Step 2 : Take a good look at our problem domains (*link to pdf*)

Step 3 : Select a domain you wish to work on. Here, you have to find a problem statement under that domain before you begin to work on a solution for it. Ex: Under the domain ‘Waste Management’, your problem statement may be ‘To provide a more feasible waste segregation system’.

step 4: Register with us at clike here

Step 5: You will receive a mail within 5 days to send an abstract about the problem statement you wish to work on.

Step 6: From here on, the Kalpana Team will be in constant touch with you for any further processes or details via e-mail.

Step 7: The best ideas will be selected and the associated teams will be allowed to participate in KALPANA.
